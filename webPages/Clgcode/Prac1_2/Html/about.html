About CHARUSAT

Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT) is a premier institution in Gujarat, India, dedicated to academic excellence, research, and innovation. Established by Shri Charotar Moti Sattavis Patidar Kelavani Mandal, CHARUSAT aims to put Charotar on the global education map.

20+ Years of Excellence

Since its inception in 2000, CHARUSAT has grown from a single institute to a multidisciplinary university with 9 institutes, 72+ programs, and a vibrant community of students and faculty.

Our Mission

To serve society through knowledge and innovation by providing world-class education and fostering research that addresses real-world challenges.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in higher education, recognized for academic excellence, research, and societal impact.
