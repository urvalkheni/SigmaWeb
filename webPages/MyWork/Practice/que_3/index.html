Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements for undergraduate programs?

For undergraduate programs, students must have completed their 12th grade with a minimum of 60% marks in relevant subjects. Additionally, students need to pass the entrance examination conducted by the college and submit all required documents including mark sheets, transfer certificate, and character certificate.

How can I apply for scholarships?

Scholarship applications can be submitted through the student portal during the specified application period. Students need to maintain a minimum GPA of 3.5 and demonstrate financial need. Merit-based scholarships are also available for top-performing students in each department.

What is the procedure for course registration?

Course registration is done online through the student portal at the beginning of each semester. Students must first meet with their academic advisor to plan their course schedule, then log into the portal during the registration window to select and confirm their courses.

Are there any hostel facilities available?

Yes, the college provides separate hostel facilities for male and female students. Each hostel room is furnished with basic amenities including bed, study table, chair, and storage space. Mess facilities provide three meals a day with both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options.

How can I access the library resources?

Students can access the library using their student ID card during operating hours (8 AM to 8 PM on weekdays, 9 AM to 5 PM on weekends). The library offers physical books, digital resources, research databases, and computer facilities. Students can borrow up to 5 books for 14 days.
